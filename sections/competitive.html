CrewAI Surges to 49K Stars as Developer Favorite

CrewAI has emerged as a developer favorite with 49,143 stars and its 1.14.2 release shows active iteration.

Its popularity stems from a clean API for building multi-agent systems where 'crews' of agents work together on tasks.

The framework's simplicity and extensive documentation have made it the go-to choice for rapid prototyping of agentic workflows.

PhiData (39,518 stars, v2.5.17) positions itself as a tool-centric agent framework, emphasizing that agents should have well-defined toolkits for interacting with external systems.
