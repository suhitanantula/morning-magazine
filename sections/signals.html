Lead Signals

BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects

SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

CAIO / Chief AI Officer Created

What it is: A new C-suite role specifically for AI has been created, signaling executive-level commitment to AI transformation.

Examples: LinkedIn announcement, press release, board filing, executive team org chart update

Why powerful: Creation of a dedicated AI leadership role indicates board-level approval and organizational structural change, not just pilot projects.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Can you tell me about your organization's AI leadership structure?
  • 🔍 Has your organization appointed a dedicated AI executive or chief AI officer?
  • 🔍 How is AI governance structured at the executive level in your company?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Explicit AI-First Public Mandate

What it is: CEO or board has explicitly stated AI as a strategic priority in public communications such as earnings calls, investor days, or press interviews.

Examples: CEO quote saying 'AI-first', 'AI transformation' in earnings call, investor day presentation, press interview

Why powerful: Public mandates create organizational urgency and are difficult to walk back. They signal that AI is not optional but a core strategic imperative.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your leadership team's public position on AI transformation?
  • 🔍 Has your CEO or board communicated an AI-first strategy externally?
  • 🔍 How is AI referenced in your recent investor or public communications?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Board Pressure / Board Directors Talking About AI

What it is: Board-level conversations about AI strategy are occurring, with AI initiatives appearing in board materials or directors discussing AI publicly.

Examples: Board announcements, director LinkedIn posts about AI, AGM discussions on AI, board meeting agenda items on AI

Why powerful: Board pressure forces executive action. When directors ask about AI, CEOs must deliver answers and results.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What board-level conversations are happening around AI strategy?
  • 🔍 Are your board directors actively engaged in AI discussions?
  • 🔍 How is AI currently featured in your board materials and strategic reviews?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

New CEO With AI Mandate

What it is: A new CEO (within 12-18 months) with a track record of AI transformation or who has signaled AI as a priority in their mandate.

Examples: CEO announcement, incoming CEO's prior AI work, mandate letters, CEO interviews on AI strategy

Why powerful: New CEOs restructure for transformation, have board backing, and have no legacy systems to protect. They are primed to make bold AI moves.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How long has your current CEO been in role?
  • 🔍 What's the new CEO's background with AI initiatives?
  • 🔍 Has your leadership change come with an explicit AI transformation mandate?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Internal AI Platform Team (Building Not Buying)

What it is: A team of 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure including RAG systems, agents, and embeddings pipelines.

Examples: Job postings for AI engineers, engineering blog posts, LinkedIn profiles of internal AI team, technical architecture announcements

Why powerful: Building internal AI platforms requires significant investment and commitment. It signals the company is moving beyond experiments to production AI.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your current internal AI engineering capability?
  • 🔍 Are you building internal AI infrastructure or relying primarily on vendors?
  • 🔍 How many engineers are currently dedicated to AI platform development?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Talent Hiring Spree

What it is: A significant increase in AI-specific hires including prompt engineers, AI architects, and ML engineers within a 6-month period.

Examples: 5+ AI-specific hires in 6 months, LinkedIn hiring signals, surge in AI-related job postings

Why powerful: Hiring sprees indicate organizational growth in AI capability and intent to scale AI initiatives beyond pilot phase.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How has your AI talent acquisition changed in the past 12 months?
  • 🔍 What AI roles have you recently added to your team?
  • 🔍 Are you currently hiring for AI-specific positions?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Process Redesign Evidence

What it is: Evidence that old workflows have been eliminated and new AI-native processes have been created.

Examples: Company blog posts about process transformation, case studies, press mentions of workflow changes, operational restructuring announcements

Why powerful: Process redesign indicates AI is driving actual business transformation, not just technology upgrades. It shows genuine organizational change.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How has AI changed your operational workflows?
  • 🔍 Have you redesigned any core business processes around AI capabilities?
  • 🔍 What traditional processes have been replaced by AI-native alternatives?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Integrator Partnerships

What it is: Working with AI consultancies or system integrators rather than just buying standalone SaaS tools.

Examples: Partner announcements, case studies with consulting firms, system integrator engagements, AI implementation partnerships

Why powerful: Engaging integrators signals the company is pursuing serious transformation, not just tool procurement. They need external expertise for complex AI initiatives.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Are you working with external AI consultants or system integrators?
  • 🔍 What AI implementation partners are currently supporting your initiatives?
  • 🔍 How are you augmenting your internal capability with external expertise?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Significant AI Budget Increase

What it is: A year-over-year increase of 50% or more in AI-related budget allocation.

Examples: Budget filings, investor presentations, internal budget announcements, CFO communications about AI investment

Why powerful: Substantial budget increases indicate commitment at the financial level. Resources follow strategy when budgets increase significantly.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How has your AI budget changed year-over-year?
  • 🔍 What level of investment are you allocating to AI transformation?
  • 🔍 Can you share your current AI budget trajectory?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Government Policy Signals

What it is: Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector, creating regulatory or market urgency.

Examples: Policy announcements, government AI strategies, regulatory changes affecting the sector, public sector AI initiatives

Why powerful: Government policy creates urgency and forces companies to respond. It also opens funding opportunities and shapes competitive dynamics.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is your sector being affected by government AI policy?
  • 🔍 Are regulatory changes in AI creating urgency for your organization?
  • 🔍 Is government funding or policy driving your AI considerations?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Industry Disruption Indicators

What it is: The company's sector is undergoing AI-driven disruption through new entrants, margin compression, or customer behavior shifts.

Examples: Analyst reports on industry disruption, trade publications, increasing industry analyst coverage, market structure changes

Why powerful: Disruption signals create urgency. When an industry is being transformed by AI, companies must adapt or risk irrelevance.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is AI disruption affecting your industry?
  • 🔍 What competitive pressures are you seeing from AI-enabled new entrants?
  • 🔍 How are customer expectations shifting due to AI in your sector?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Competitor AI Moves

What it is: Key competitors are announcing AI initiatives or gaining market share through AI-powered capabilities.

Examples: Competitor press releases, industry news, market share data, analyst comparisons of AI capabilities

Why powerful: Competitor success with AI creates copycat pressure. When competitors win with AI, others must respond.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What AI initiatives are your competitors pursuing?
  • 🔍 Have competitors gained advantage through AI capabilities?
  • 🔍 How is AI affecting your competitive positioning?
