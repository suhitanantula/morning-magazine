Lead Signals

BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects

SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

CAIO / Chief AI Officer Created

What it is: New C-suite role specifically created for AI leadership, indicating board-level commitment to AI transformation

Examples: LinkedIn announcement, press release, board filing, executive team org chart update

Why powerful: New C-suite roles require board approval and represent structural commitment. CAIO signals AI is a strategic priority, not an IT project. These companies typically have budget and mandate for transformation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Can you tell me about your current AI leadership structure? Have you appointed anyone specifically focused on AI strategy?
  • 🔍 How is AI positioned in your executive team? Who owns the AI transformation mandate?
  • 🔍 Has your board discussed AI strategy recently? What's the governance around AI decisions?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Explicit AI-First Public Mandate

What it is: CEO, board, or senior leadership has publicly stated AI as a top strategic priority in earnings calls, investor days, or press interviews

Examples: Earnings call transcripts, investor day presentations, press interviews, CEO blog posts, annual report letters

Why powerful: Public commitments create accountability. When CEOs say AI is a priority in front of investors, they must deliver. This signals budget allocation and executive attention.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your CEO's public stance on AI? Has leadership communicated AI as a strategic priority?
  • 🔍 In your investor communications, how is AI being positioned?
  • 🔍 What has your leadership team said about AI transformation externally?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Board Pressure / Board Directors Talking About AI

What it is: Board-level conversations about AI strategy appearing in board materials, AGM discussions, or board directors publicly discussing AI

Examples: Board announcements, director LinkedIn posts about AI, AGM AI discussions, board committee creation for AI

Why powerful: Board pressure is the most powerful forcing function in enterprise. When directors ask about AI, CEOs must respond. This creates urgency and budget that trickles down.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Is AI a topic that comes up in your board discussions? What's the board's appetite for AI investment?
  • 🔍 Are your board directors asking about AI strategy? How is that being responded to?
  • 🔍 Has your board formed any committees or working groups focused on AI?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

New CEO with AI Mandate

What it is: New CEO (within 12-18 months) with track record of AI transformation or who has explicitly signaled AI as priority

Examples: CEO announcement, incoming CEO's prior AI work, mandate letters, CEO's first 100 days communications

Why powerful: New CEOs restructure organisations, have strong board backing, and don't have legacy systems to protect. They often bring in new leadership and are more open to external partners.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How long has your current CEO been in role? What's their background with AI?
  • 🔍 As a new leader, what strategic priorities has your CEO highlighted?
  • 🔍 Has the CEO signalled any specific transformation initiatives since joining?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Internal AI Platform Team Building

What it is: Evidence of 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure including RAG systems, agents, embeddings pipelines, or custom AI platforms

Examples: Job postings for AI engineers, engineering blog posts, LinkedIn profiles of internal AI team, technical architecture announcements

Why powerful: Companies building internal AI platforms are making significant investment. They're sophisticated enough to know they need AI capability but may still need strategic guidance on roadmap and prioritisation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your current AI engineering capability? How many engineers are working on AI initiatives?
  • 🔍 Are you building internal AI infrastructure or primarily buying? What's the approach?
  • 🔍 Can you tell me about your AI tech stack and platform investments?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Talent Hiring Spree

What it is: Significant increase in AI-specific hires including prompt engineers, AI architects, ML engineers, data scientists - 5+ hires in 6 months

Examples: LinkedIn hiring signals, job posting surge, recruitment announcements, talent acquisition press releases

Why powerful: Hiring sprees indicate budget approval and strategic intent. Multiple hires suggest sustained investment, not experimental pilots.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How has your AI talent acquisition changed recently? Are you hiring for AI roles?
  • 🔍 What's your current headcount in AI-related roles? How has that grown?
  • 🔍 What AI roles are you prioritising in your hiring right now?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Process Redesign Evidence

What it is: Evidence of eliminating old workflows and creating new AI-native processes, not just adding AI features to existing workflows

Examples: Company blog posts about transformation, case studies, press mentions of workflow changes, internal communications about new processes

Why powerful: True process redesign indicates transformative intent, not just automation of existing tasks. This signals readiness for deeper AI integration.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Have you redesigned any core business processes around AI capabilities?
  • 🔍 What's changed in how your teams work since introducing AI? Are there new workflows?
  • 🔍 Can you point to specific processes that have been transformed, not just digitised?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Integrator Partnerships

What it is: Working with AI consultancies, system integrators, or advisory firms rather than just buying SaaS tools

Examples: Partner announcements, case studies with consulting firms, implementation partner press releases

Why powerful: Companies working with integrators are serious about transformation. They recognise they need external expertise and are willing to invest in guided implementation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Are you working with any external AI advisors or implementation partners?
  • 🔍 What does your current AI partner ecosystem look like?
  • 🔍 Have you engaged consultants or system integrators for AI work?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Significant AI Budget Increase

What it is: 50%+ year-over-year increase in AI budget, indicating strategic commitment and resource allocation

Examples: Budget filings, investor presentations, internal budget communications, CFO announcements

Why powerful: Budget increases are leading indicators of transformation intent. 50%+ YOY growth suggests AI is moving from experimental to strategic.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How has your AI budget evolved? What's the year-over-year change?
  • 🔍 What's the current budget allocation for AI initiatives?
  • 🔍 Are you seeing increased investment in AI compared to previous periods?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Government Policy Signals

What it is: Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector, creating urgency and forcing response

Examples: Policy announcements, government AI strategies, regulatory changes, sector-specific AI mandates

Why powerful: Government policy creates external urgency that bypasses internal resistance. Regulatory changes force companies to act quickly, often with external help.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is regulatory change in AI affecting your planning? Are there policy drivers in your sector?
  • 🔍 Has government policy created urgency for AI transformation in your organisation?
  • 🔍 Are there compliance or regulatory pressures driving your AI timeline?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Industry Disruption Indicators

What it is: Company's sector is undergoing AI-driven disruption through new entrants, margin compression, or customer behaviour shifts

Examples: Analyst reports, trade publications, increasing industry analyst coverage, market disruption news

Why powerful: Disruption creates motivation. Companies facing competitive pressure are more likely to invest in AI transformation and less likely to delay.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is AI changing the competitive landscape in your sector? What are you seeing?
  • 🔍 Are new entrants or market shifts forcing you to reconsider your technology strategy?
  • 🔍 What's the urgency around AI in your industry? Are competitors pulling ahead?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Competitor AI Moves

What it is: Key competitors announcing AI initiatives, gaining market share through AI advantage, or demonstrating AI-powered capabilities

Examples: Competitor press releases, industry news coverage, market share data, competitor case studies

Why powerful: Competitor activity creates FOMO and validates the market. When competitors move, boards ask questions and budgets open.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What are your competitors doing with AI? Are you seeing them pull ahead?
  • 🔍 How does your AI readiness compare to peers? What are others in your sector doing?
  • 🔍 Is competitive pressure driving your AI conversations internally?
