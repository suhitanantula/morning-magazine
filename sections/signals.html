Lead Signals

BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects

SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

CAIO / Chief AI Officer Created

What it is: New C-suite role specifically for AI

Examples: LinkedIn announcement, press release, board filing

Why powerful: Creation of a dedicated C-suite role signals board-level commitment and organizational structurally prioritizes AI transformation

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 When did you establish your Chief AI Officer role and what's the mandate?
  • 🔍 How is the CAIO positioned relative to other C-suite leaders?
  • 🔍 What budget and team size does the CAIO have access to?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Explicit AI-First Public Mandate

What it is: CEO or board explicitly said 'AI-first' or 'AI transformation' in public communications

Examples: Earnings call, investor day, press interview, annual report statement

Why powerful: Public commitments create accountability and signal genuine strategic intent beyond vague aspirations

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Can you share the specific AI vision you've communicated externally?
  • 🔍 How are you measuring progress against your AI-first commitment?
  • 🔍 What resources have you allocated to support this public mandate?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Board Pressure / Board Directors Talking About AI

What it is: Board-level conversations about AI strategy, AI initiatives in board materials

Examples: Board announcements, director LinkedIn posts about AI, AGM AI discussions, board committee charters

Why powerful: Board pressure forces executive action and creates urgency with guaranteed executive attention

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How frequently does your board review AI strategy and progress?
  • 🔍 What specific AI directives has your board communicated this year?
  • 🔍 How is board-level pressure being translated into operational execution?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

New CEO with AI Mandate

What it is: New CEO (within 12-18 months) with track record of AI transformation or AI as priority

Examples: CEO announcement, incoming CEO's prior AI work, mandate letters, executive bios

Why powerful: New CEOs restructure for transformation, have board backing, and no legacy systems to protect

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What AI transformation experience does your new CEO bring from previous roles?
  • 🔍 How has the CEO's appointment accelerated your AI strategic priorities?
  • 🔍 What specific AI mandates has the new CEO communicated to the organisation?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Internal AI Platform Team (Building Not Buying)

What it is: 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure (RAG, agents, embeddings pipelines)

Examples: Job postings, engineering blog, LinkedIn technical posts, technical documentation

Why powerful: Investment in internal platform teams signals long-term commitment and capability building beyond point solutions

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's the size and structure of your internal AI platform team?
  • 🔍 What internal AI infrastructure are you building—RAG systems, agents, embeddings?
  • 🔍 How does your internal team collaborate with business units on AI initiatives?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Talent Hiring Spree

What it is: 5+ AI-specific hires in 6 months (prompt engineers, AI architects, ML engineers)

Examples: LinkedIn hiring signals, job posting surge, recruitment announcements

Why powerful: Aggressive talent acquisition demonstrates commitment to building internal AI capabilities at scale

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How many AI-specific roles have you hired in the past 6 months?
  • 🔍 What AI talent categories are you prioritising in your hiring strategy?
  • 🔍 How is your AI hiring pipeline supporting your transformation goals?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Process Redesign Evidence

What it is: Eliminated old workflows, created new AI-native processes

Examples: Company blog, case study, press mention about workflow transformation

Why powerful: Process redesign shows operational commitment to AI-driven transformation beyond technology adoption

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What legacy processes have you redesigned or eliminated due to AI?
  • 🔍 How are you measuring the impact of AI-driven process changes?
  • 🔍 What new AI-native processes have you implemented across the organisation?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI Integrator Partnerships

What it is: Working with AI consultancies or system integrators (not just buying SaaS tools)

Examples: Partner announcements, case studies with consulting firms, joint solution launches

Why powerful: Working with specialised partners indicates serious investment and recognition of need for external expertise

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Which AI consulting or system integration partners are you working with?
  • 🔍 What specific AI initiatives are you collaborating on with external partners?
  • 🔍 How are you selecting and managing your AI implementation partners?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Significant AI Budget Increase

What it is: 50%+ YOY increase in AI budget

Examples: Budget filings, investor presentations, financial disclosures, internal budget documents

Why powerful: Substantial budget increases demonstrate financial commitment and organisational prioritisation of AI

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's been the year-over-year growth in your AI budget?
  • 🔍 How is your AI budget allocated across different initiative categories?
  • 🔍 What ROI metrics are you tracking to justify continued AI investment?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Government Policy Signals

What it is: Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector

Examples: Policy announcements, government AI strategies, regulatory changes, funding programs

Why powerful: Creates urgency, forces companies to respond, and opens funding opportunities

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How are recent government AI policies affecting your industry?
  • 🔍 What regulatory changes are you preparing for in your AI strategy?
  • 🔍 Are you accessing any government AI funding or incentive programs?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Industry Disruption Indicators

What it is: Company's sector undergoing AI-driven disruption (new entrants, margin compression, customer behaviour shifts)

Examples: Analyst reports, trade publications, industry analyst coverage, market research

Why powerful: Disruption signals create strategic urgency and justify transformation investments to stakeholders

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is AI-driven disruption affecting your industry right now?
  • 🔍 What new market entrants are threatening your business model?
  • 🔍 How are customer expectations shifting due to AI in your sector?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Competitor AI Moves

What it is: Key competitors announcing AI initiatives, gaining market share through AI

Examples: Competitor press releases, industry news, market share data, competitive analysis

Why powerful: Competitor activity validates market urgency and creates pressure to match or exceed AI capabilities

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Which competitors have you noticed making significant AI investments?
  • 🔍 How are competitor AI initiatives impacting your market position?
  • 🔍 What's your timeline to match competitor AI capabilities?
