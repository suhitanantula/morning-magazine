🎯 Lead Signals  · BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects
CAIO / Chief AI Officer Created
STRONG

New C-suite role specifically created for AI leadership, signaling strategic prioritisation at the highest level.

  • When did you establish your Chief AI Officer role, and what's the mandate?
  • How is the CAIO structured to report — directly to CEO or CTO?
  • What budget authority does the CAIO have?
Explicit AI-First Public Mandate
STRONG

CEO or board has explicitly stated AI as a strategic priority in public communications such as earnings calls, investor days, or press interviews.

  • Can you share how your CEO articulated the AI strategy publicly?
  • What's the timeline attached to your AI-first commitment?
  • How is the board measuring progress against this mandate?
Board Pressure / Board Directors Talking About AI
STRONG

Board-level conversations about AI strategy appearing in board materials, AGM discussions, or board director LinkedIn activity.

  • How frequently is AI appearing on your board agenda?
  • Which board directors are driving the AI conversation?
  • What board-level AI initiatives have been approved recently?
New CEO with AI Mandate
STRONG

A new CEO (appointed within 12-18 months) with a track record of AI transformation or who has signalled AI as a priority in their mandate.

  • What's your CEO's vision for AI transformation?
  • How does the board's mandate to the CEO reference AI?
  • What legacy systems is the new leadership looking to replace?
Internal AI Platform Team (Building Not Buying)
STRONG

Evidence of 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure including RAG systems, agents, and embedding pipelines.

  • What's the size and structure of your internal AI platform team?
  • What internal AI infrastructure are you building?
  • Where are the gaps where external partners could accelerate delivery?
AI Talent Hiring Spree
MEDIUM

Significant increase in AI-specific hires including prompt engineers, AI architects, and ML engineers within a 6-month period.

  • How many AI roles have you hired in the last 6 months?
  • What's your AI talent strategy — build, buy, or partner?
  • Where are the biggest gaps in your AI capability right now?
Process Redesign Evidence
MEDIUM

Evidence of eliminated old workflows and creation of new AI-native processes documented in company communications.

  • Which processes have you redesigned around AI?
  • What's the ROI you've seen from process transformation?
  • Which workflows are still manual that you'd like to automate?
AI Integrator Partnerships
MEDIUM

Working with AI consultancies or system integrators rather than just purchasing SaaS tools — indicates a strategic rather than tactical approach.

  • Which AI partners and integrators are you working with?
  • What has been the outcome of those partnerships?
  • Where have you found gaps in external support?
Significant AI Budget Increase
MEDIUM

Year-over-year increase of 50% or more in AI-related spending, indicating strategic commitment and resource allocation.

  • What's your YOY AI budget growth?
  • How is your AI budget allocated across build vs buy vs partner?
  • What stops you from investing more?
Government Policy Signals
MEDIUM

Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector, creating urgency and regulatory response requirements.

  • How is recent government policy affecting your AI strategy?
  • Are there regulatory compliance requirements driving your AI investment?
  • What government support programs are you accessing?
Industry Disruption Indicators
MEDIUM

The company's sector is experiencing AI-driven disruption through new entrants, margin compression, or customer behaviour shifts.

  • How is AI disrupting your industry right now?
  • What's the competitive threat from AI-native entrants?
  • Which competitor moves are forcing your hand?
Competitor AI Moves
MEDIUM

Key competitors announcing AI initiatives, gaining market share through AI advantages, or publicly committing to AI transformation.

  • Which competitors are you watching on AI?
  • What's driving urgency relative to competitor activity?
  • What's your competitive differentiation strategy against AI-enabled rivals?
