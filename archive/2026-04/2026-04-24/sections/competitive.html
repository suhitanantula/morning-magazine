MetaGPT surges to 67K stars as multi-agent simulation gains traction

MetaGPT has emerged as the highest-starred multi-agent framework at 67,364 stars, recently releasing v0.8.2.

Unlike traditional agent frameworks, MetaGPT simulates entire software companies with specialized AI roles collaborating on complex tasks.

This approach represents a distinct architectural philosophy from The Helix Lab's focus on autonomous research agents, suggesting market validation for role-based multi-agent systems that compete for similar enterprise use cases.
