Q1 2026 AI Funding Hits $300B — 4x Year Over Year

73% of Enterprises Fail to Achieve Intended Benefits from First AI Implementation

60% of AI projects never progress beyond pilot phase.

Primary causes include lack of executive sponsorship, insufficient data governance, unclear accountability, inadequate risk assessment, and missing continuous monitoring frameworks.

Only 15% of companies report having mature AI governance frameworks.

The 77% organizational failure rate (versus 23% technical) reveals the problem isn't better models—it's measurement frameworks, business ownership, and change management.
