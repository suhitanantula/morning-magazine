Lead Signals

BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects

SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

CAIO / Chief AI Officer created

What it is: New C-suite role specifically for AI indicates formalised AI strategy and executive-level commitment to transformation.

Examples: LinkedIn announcement, press release, board filing, executive appointment press

Why powerful: Creates a dedicated leader with budget authority and board access - signals organisation is serious about AI beyond experimentation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 When did you create your Chief AI Officer role, and what's their mandate?
  • 🔍 What's the CAIO's budget authority and reporting line to the board?
  • 🔍 How is the CAIO measuring success in the first 90 days?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Explicit AI-first public mandate

What it is: CEO or board has explicitly stated AI as a strategic priority in public communications such as earnings calls, investor days, or press interviews.

Examples: Earnings call transcript, investor day presentation, press interview, CEO blog post

Why powerful: Public mandates create accountability and signal resource allocation is coming - leadership cannot easily walk back public commitments.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What does 'AI-first' mean for your organisation specifically?
  • 🔍 How is the CEO communicating AI priorities to the organisation?
  • 🔍 What's the timeline for the AI transformation mandate?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Board pressure / Board directors talking about AI

What it is: Board-level conversations about AI strategy appearing in board materials, director LinkedIn posts, or AGM discussions.

Examples: Board announcements, director LinkedIn posts about AI, AGM AI discussions, board strategy documents

Why powerful: Board pressure forces executive action - directors are asking questions that require answers, creating urgency at the highest level.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How frequently is AI appearing on your board agenda?
  • 🔍 What questions are board members asking about AI strategy?
  • 🔍 Has the board commissioned any AI-specific reviews or strategy sessions?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

New CEO with AI mandate

What it is: New CEO (within 12-18 months) with a track record of AI transformation or who has signalled AI as a priority in their mandate.

Examples: CEO announcement, incoming CEO's prior AI work, mandate letters, CEO vision statement

Why powerful: New CEOs restructure for transformation, have board backing, and have no legacy systems to protect - they are looking for quick wins to establish credibility.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your vision for AI transformation in the first 12 months?
  • 🔍 Do you have board backing for a significant AI investment?
  • 🔍 What legacy systems or processes are you looking to transform first?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Internal AI platform team (building not buying)

What it is: 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure including RAG systems, agents, and embeddings pipelines.

Examples: Job postings, engineering blog, LinkedIn, technical architecture documentation

Why powerful: Building internal platforms signals long-term commitment and technical sophistication - these teams need strategic partners, not just vendors.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your internal AI platform architecture looking like?
  • 🔍 How many engineers are currently on your AI platform team?
  • 🔍 What are the biggest technical challenges your platform team is facing?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI talent hiring spree

What it is: 5+ AI-specific hires in 6 months including prompt engineers, AI architects, and ML engineers.

Examples: LinkedIn hiring signals, job posting surge, recruitment announcements

Why powerful: Rapid AI hiring indicates scaling intent - the organisation is moving beyond pilot phase and needs people to execute.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's driving your AI hiring surge - new projects or replacing attrition?
  • 🔍 Which AI roles are hardest to fill and why?
  • 🔍 How are you integrating new AI hires into existing teams?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Process redesign evidence

What it is: Evidence of eliminated old workflows and creation of new AI-native processes.

Examples: Company blog, case study, press mention, internal documentation

Why powerful: Process redesign indicates real transformation, not just technology adoption - the organisation is changing how work gets done.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Which processes have you redesigned for AI?
  • 🔍 What's the ROI you're seeing from process redesign?
  • 🔍 How are you managing change management alongside process changes?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI integrator partnerships

What it is: Working with AI consultancies or system integrators rather than just buying SaaS tools.

Examples: Partner announcements, case studies with consulting firms, joint press releases

Why powerful: Working with integrators signals complexity and need for external expertise - they recognise they can't do this alone.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Which AI partners are you working with currently?
  • 🔍 What gap are you hiring integrators to fill?
  • 🔍 What's your criteria for selecting AI strategy partners?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Significant AI budget increase

What it is: 50%+ year-over-year increase in AI budget.

Examples: Budget filings, investor presentations, internal budget documents

Why powerful: Budget increases indicate commitment at the financial level - someone has approved significant spend for AI initiatives.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's driving your 50%+ AI budget increase?
  • 🔍 How is your AI budget allocated between build vs buy vs partners?
  • 🔍 What's the approval process for AI investment beyond the planned budget?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Government policy signals

What it is: Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector, creating regulatory or market urgency.

Examples: Policy announcements, government AI strategies, regulatory changes, sector-specific AI frameworks

Why powerful: Government policy creates urgency and forces companies to respond - can unlock funding and accelerate decision-making.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is recent government AI policy affecting your sector?
  • 🔍 Are you pursuing any government AI funding or incentives?
  • 🔍 How are you preparing for upcoming regulatory requirements?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Industry disruption indicators

What it is: Company's sector is undergoing AI-driven disruption through new entrants, margin compression, or customer behaviour shifts.

Examples: Analyst reports, trade publications, industry analyst coverage, market research

Why powerful: Disruption signals create urgency and justify transformation investment - leadership can point to external pressure as motivation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is AI disrupting your specific sector right now?
  • 🔍 What's the competitive threat from new AI-native entrants?
  • 🔍 How are customer expectations changing due to AI in your industry?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Competitor AI moves

What it is: Key competitors announcing AI initiatives or gaining market share through AI advantages.

Examples: Competitor press releases, industry news, market share data, analyst coverage

Why powerful: Competitor success creates FOMO and validates the opportunity - easier to get approval when others are succeeding.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Which competitors are you watching on AI, and what are they doing?
  • 🔍 Have competitors gained any market share advantage through AI?
  • 🔍 What lessons have you learned from competitor AI initiatives?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

New AI features announced

What it is: Product updates featuring AI capabilities, typically from product or engineering teams.

Examples: Product launch announcements, feature release notes, app updates

Why powerful: Product AI features indicate innovation but not necessarily organisational transformation - useful context but low qualification value.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How are your AI product features being received by customers?
  • 🔍 Are AI features driving new customer acquisition or retention?
  • 🔍 What's your product roadmap for AI capabilities?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI training programs

What it is: Internal upskilling programs for employees on AI tools and concepts.

Examples: L&D announcements, training schedules, internal communications

Why powerful: Training indicates cultural awareness but without process change it's just upskilling - not transformation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How many employees have completed AI training?
  • 🔍 Are you measuring productivity gains from AI training?
  • 🔍 How does training connect to actual AI adoption in workflows?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI task force / committee

What it is: Cross-functional teams or committees formed to explore or coordinate AI initiatives.

Examples: Internal announcements, meeting minutes, steering committee formation

Why powerful: Task forces indicate exploration but often lack budget authority - need to check if they have real power.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's the mandate and budget authority of your AI task force?
  • 🔍 How often does the task force meet and report to leadership?
  • 🔍 What has the task force achieved in the last 6 months?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Single AI hire

What it is: Addition of one AI-focused role without broader structural change.

Examples: LinkedIn hire, job posting for single role

Why powerful: Single hires may be experiments or box-ticking - need to see if part of larger strategy.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What was the rationale for this specific hire?
  • 🔍 How is this role connected to broader AI strategy?
  • 🔍 Are more AI hires planned?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI mentioned in annual report

What it is: AI referenced in annual reports or statutory filings, often in strategic outlook sections.

Examples: Annual report, 10-K, ESG report, sustainability filing

Why powerful: Box-ticking compliance mention - low signal value but can indicate awareness at board level.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's the depth of AI commitment beyond the annual report mention?
  • 🔍 Are there specific AI initiatives funded in this year's plan?
  • 🔍 How does AI feature in your investor communication strategy?
