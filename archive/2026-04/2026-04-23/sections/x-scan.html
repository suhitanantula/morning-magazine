Australia's AI Safety Institute Now Operational with $29.9M Funding

Australia launched the Australian AI Safety Institute in November 2025 with $29.9M funding, becoming operational in early 2026 as an advisory body focused on pre-deployment testing of advanced AI systems.

The AISI complements existing regulators including OAIC, ACCC, and eSafety while identifying gaps where current laws fail to address AI-specific risks.

The government has shifted toward lighter-touch regulatory strategy focused on safety evaluation rather than prescriptive rules.
