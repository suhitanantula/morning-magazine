Lead Signals

BD signal framework for qualifying AI transformation prospects

SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

CAIO / Chief AI Officer created

What it is: New C-suite role specifically for AI - indicates formalised AI strategy at executive level

Examples: LinkedIn announcement, press release, board filing, executive hiring press

Why powerful: Creation of dedicated AI leadership signals board-level commitment and budget allocation. This role typically reports to CEO/COO, meaning AI is treated as a strategic priority, not an IT project.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is your CAIO/AI leader positioned in the org structure and who do they report to?
  • 🔍 What's the mandate and budget scope for your new AI leadership role?
  • 🔍 How quickly did you move from recognizing the need for AI leadership to actually hiring?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Explicit AI-first public mandate

What it is: CEO or board explicitly stated AI as strategic priority in public communications

Examples: Earnings call remarks, investor day presentations, press interviews, annual report statements, CEO LinkedIn posts

Why powerful: Public commitments create accountability. When a CEO says AI is a priority to investors or in press, they must deliver. This signals real budget and organisational focus, not just internal discussion.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 Can you tell me about the AI mandate that was communicated internally and externally?
  • 🔍 How has that public commitment translated into resource allocation and timelines?
  • 🔍 What does 'AI-first' mean specifically for your organisation's strategy?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Board pressure / Board directors talking about AI

What it is: Board-level conversations about AI strategy appearing in board materials or board member communications

Examples: Board announcements, director LinkedIn posts about AI, AGM AI discussions, board committee creation for AI

Why powerful: Board pressure forces executive action. When directors are asking about AI, CEOs must respond. This creates urgency and typically unlocks budget that wouldn't otherwise be available.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How frequently is AI appearing on your board agenda currently?
  • 🔍 What specific board-level discussions have happened around AI strategy?
  • 🔍 How is the board measuring progress on AI initiatives?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

New CEO with AI mandate

What it is: New CEO (within 12-18 months) with track record of AI transformation or who has signalled AI as priority

Examples: CEO announcement mentioning AI, incoming CEO's prior AI work, mandate letters, CEO vision statements

Why powerful: New CEOs restructure for transformation, have board backing, and no legacy systems to protect. They are looking for quick wins to establish credibility and AI is a common lever.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's your vision for AI transformation in your first 12-18 months?
  • 🔍 What legacy systems or processes are you most eager to transform with AI?
  • 🔍 How much board backing do you have for aggressive AI investment?
SIGNAL STRONG SIGNAL

Internal AI platform team (building not buying)

What it is: 10+ engineers building internal AI infrastructure - RAG systems, agents, embeddings pipelines, custom LLMOps

Examples: Job postings for platform engineers, engineering blog posts, LinkedIn about internal AI stack, GitHub activity, technical architecture announcements

Why powerful: Building rather than buying signals sophisticated AI ambition. This requires significant investment and signals the company views AI as core competitive advantage, not a point solution.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's driving your decision to build rather than buy AI capabilities?
  • 🔍 How large is your internal AI platform team and what's their roadmap?
  • 🔍 What specific technical infrastructure are you building?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI talent hiring spree

What it is: 5+ AI-specific hires in 6 months including prompt engineers, AI architects, ML engineers

Examples: LinkedIn hiring signals, job posting surge, recruitment announcements, talent market activity

Why powerful: Rapid AI hiring indicates serious investment. While hiring alone doesn't guarantee transformation, clustered AI hires suggest structured AI strategy and budget approval.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What's driving the recent AI hiring push - new projects or replacing attrition?
  • 🔍 How are you competing for AI talent in the current market?
  • 🔍 What's the ratio of AI roles to total engineering headcount growth?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Process redesign evidence

What it is: Evidence of eliminated old workflows and creation of new AI-native processes

Examples: Company blog posts, case studies, press mentions, internal comms about new ways of working, workflow automation announcements

Why powerful: Process change distinguishes real transformation from pilot fatigue. Companies redesigning workflows are moving beyond experiments to operational change - a strong indicator of readiness for strategic partnership.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What existing processes have you redesigned or retired as part of your AI adoption?
  • 🔍 How are you measuring the impact of process changes?
  • 🔍 What's the hardest process transformation you've undertaken so far?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

AI integrator partnerships

What it is: Working with AI consultancies or system integrators rather than just buying SaaS tools

Examples: Partner announcements, case studies with consulting firms, system integrator engagements, AI advisory engagements

Why powerful: Engaging integrators signals budget and recognition that transformation requires expertise beyond internal capability. These companies are serious about implementation, not just evaluation.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 What AI partners or integrators are you working with currently?
  • 🔍 How are you thinking about build vs partner vs buy for your AI capabilities?
  • 🔍 What's been the biggest gap between AI tool promises and implementation reality?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Significant AI budget increase

What it is: 50%+ Year-over-Year increase in AI budget

Examples: Budget filings, investor presentations, internal budget communications, CFO statements

Why powerful: Budget increases indicate strategic commitment. A 50%+ jump suggests AI has moved from pilot budget to strategic investment category. This also signals willingness to spend on external expertise.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How did you arrive at your current AI budget allocation?
  • 🔍 What's the split between operational AI spend vs transformation investment?
  • 🔍 How flexible is your AI budget for unexpected opportunities?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Government policy signals

What it is: Politicians or bureaucrats making AI announcements that affect the company's sector

Examples: Policy announcements, government AI strategies, regulatory changes, sector-specific AI mandates

Why powerful: Government action creates urgency and forces companies to respond. Policy shifts often come with funding opportunities or compliance requirements that accelerate AI adoption timelines.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is regulatory or policy change in your sector affecting your AI timeline?
  • 🔍 Are there government incentives or funding programs you're accessing for AI adoption?
  • 🔍 How are you preparing for sector-specific AI regulations?
SIGNAL MEDIUM SIGNAL

Industry disruption indicators

What it is: Company's sector undergoing AI-driven disruption through new entrants, margin compression, customer behaviour shifts

Examples: Analyst reports, trade publications, industry analyst coverage, market research, competitive landscape changes

Why powerful: Disruption creates motivation. When a sector is being transformed by AI, companies must respond or risk obsolescence. This urgency makes them more receptive to BD conversations.

🎯 BD DISCOVERY QUESTIONS
  • 🔍 How is AI-driven disruption changing your competitive landscape?
  • 🔍 What specific competitive threats are driving your AI investment?
  • 🔍 How do you see your sector evolving in the next 3-5 years with AI?

Competitor AI moves
