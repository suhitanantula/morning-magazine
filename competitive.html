Competitive Landscape

Open-source multi-agent frameworks are racing to capture developer mindshare, with MetaGPT and AutoGen leading star velocity while newer entrants like Holaboss target specific workspace niches.

MetaGPT has surged to 67,364 stars on GitHub, positioning itself as the leading multi-agent framework for software company simulation.

The v0.8.2 release indicates active development of complex agent orchestration capabilities.

Its architecture allows multiple AI agents to collaborate as a virtual software team, attracting developers building sophisticated automation pipelines.
